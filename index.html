Fostering artistic and social cohesion among undergraduate pianists at Yale

What we do

YUPC is a community of pianists at Yale College who convene regularly to perform for and with each other. We seek to learn from one another musically and make friends who share our passion for piano. While doing all of this, we have a great time!

Learn more

Join YUPC!

YUPC strives to be a welcoming organization, and all undergraduate pianists are invited to apply! The application process, which takes place each fall, is designed to be manageable and rewarding.
