Gigs Around Yale

A regularly updated listing of opportunities for undergraduate pianists, both on campus and around New Haven

Opera Theatre of Yale College

OTYC is searching for capable pianists to act as accompanists and vocal coaches for its fall mainstage, Gallantry by Douglas Moore. Performances will take place October 6-8. If interested, contact Jack Lindberg at jacob.lindberg@yale.edu. [Posted August 26, 2017.]

Slifka Center for Jewish Life

The Slifka Center for Jewish life needs a pianist for its Reform high holidays services this year. This is a paid position open to anyone, regardless of religious affiliation. There will be two or three rehearsals. Services themselves will occur the night of Sept. 20, the morning of Sept. 21, the night of Sept. 29, and the morning of Sept. 30. If interested, contact Joe Blumberg at joseph.blumberg@yale.edu. [Posted August 26, 2017.]
