Piano Calendar

A listing of performances, masterclasses, and opportunities for undergraduate pianists, both on campus and around New Haven

Events marked with an asterisk (*) are YUPC-sponsored. Studio classes take place each month, and they are posted here whenever we are able to finalize the location and time with the YSM Operations office. To post an event, fill out the form below.

YUPC Movie Night!*

April 2, 7 PM
Leigh 402
Masterclass with Duo Amadeae
Esther and Sun-a Park, winners of the Chicago International Duo Piano Competition, teach YUPC students in a masterclasss.

Students of Boris Berman in Recital

April 4, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
There are only a few teachers in the world who can literally make an entire recital program from the subset of their students who end up being really famous. Boris Berman is one of those. Tickets available here.

Masterclass with Hung-Kuan Chen

April 5, 10:30 AM
Sprague Hall
YSM professor Hung-Kuan Chen teaches graduate students in a masterclass. If you'd like to observe, you can enter through the backstage entrance at 98 Wall.

April Studio Class*

April 7, 4:15 PM
Leigh 402
Our monthly gathering at which members of YUPC play for each other and receive feedback.

Masterclass with Amy Lin

April 12, 10:30 AM
Sprague Hall
Professor Amy Lin of the Conservatory of Strasbourg visits to teach YSM graduate students in a masterclass. If you'd like to observe, you can enter through the backstage entrance at 98 Wall.

Duets & Donuts: Bulldog Days Edition!*

April 24, 10:30 PM
Hopper Common Room
YUPC hosts its annual Bulldog Days Edition of Duets & Donuts, featuring delectable donuts from Donut Crazy, duet sight-reading, and propsective first years!! Come help us show the prefrosh why Yale is the best place ever for pianists!

Masterclass with Melvin Chen
