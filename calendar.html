Piano Calendar

A listing of performances, masterclasses, and opportunities for undergraduate pianists, both on campus and around New Haven

Events marked with an asterisk (*) are YUPC-sponsored. Studio classes take place each month, and they are posted here whenever we are able to finalize the location and time with the YSM Operations office.

Wei-Yi Yang in Recital

Nov 29, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
Wei-Yi Yang, a professor at YSM, performs Schubert's "Gasteiner" Sonata, Bach, and more. Tickets available here.

Masterclass with Melvin Chen

Dec 13, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
YSM Deputy Dean Melvin Chen will teach a masterclass to YSM students. You can observe for free! Enter through the backstage entrance at 98 Wall Street.

December Studio Class*

Dec 2, 4 PM
Leigh 402
Bring a piece you've been working on recently or an old staple. Perform for your peers, hear them play their own repertoire, and exchange feedback in a relaxed and friendly environment. Performers are welcome to use the music if necessary.

Duets and Donuts*

Dec 8, 7 PM
Location TBA
Come enjoy some Donut Crazy and four-hand duets with fellow pianists!

Hung-Kuan Chen in Recital

Dec 13, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
Renowned pianist and pedagogue Hung-Kuan Chen will perform live in Sprague Hall. (Prof. Chen contracted focal dystonia in the early 1990s and has a pretty remarkable story to tell about it — a very worthwhile read!). Tickets available here.

Masterclass with Hung-Kuan Chen

Dec 13, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
Prof. Chen will teach a masterclass to YSM students. You can observe for free! Enter through the backstage entrance at 98 Wall Street.

Melvin Chen in Recital

Jan 17, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
Yale College alum and YSM Deputy Dean Melvin Chen will perform in recital. (Yes, he also has a master's degree in violin from Juilliard and a Ph.D. in chemistry from Harvard, but don't worry, this is still just a piano recital.) Tickets available here.

Suites & Sweets*

February 10
Location TBA
In this collaborative performance, YUPC presents some of the rich literature of dances arranged for multiple pianists. Sweets will be present in abundance for all who attend. Free admission.

Lecture: Interpreting Chopin's Notational Indications

Feb 13, 5 PM
Pianist, composer, and scholar Seymour Bernstein will give a lecture based on his book "Chopin: Interpreting His Notational Symbols." Free admission.

Marc-André Hamelin in Recital

Feb 14, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
Renowned Canadian pianist and composer Marc-André Hamelin will perform (per usual) a ridiculously hard program, this time consisting of Liszt, Feinberg, and Debussy, and Godowsky. Tickets available here.

Robert Blocker in Recital

Mar 7, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
YSM Dean Robert Blocker performs live! Tickets available here.

Students of Boris Berman in Recital

Apr 4, 7:30 PM
Sprague Hall
There are only a few teachers in the world who can literally make an entire recital program from the subset of their students who end up being really famous. Boris Berman is one of those. Tickets available here.

First-year Showcase Concert*

Early April
Location TBA
The wonderful first-years in YUPC will have the opportunity to perform in recital by themselves in this annual tradition. Free admission.
